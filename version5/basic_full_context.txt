### Primary AHU Data (Pattern: `ahu_[unit_code]_om_p`)
- Description: Key-value table for AHU sensor readings. Use `param_id` and `param_value` columns.
- Columns: id, measured_time, param_id, param_value, created_at, modified_at
- Key Parameters (`param_id`):
  - `OAT`: Outside Air Temperature.
  - `SAT`: Supply Air Temperature.
  - `RAT`: Return Air Temperature.
  - `MAT`: Mixed Air Temperature.
  - `SAT_SP`: Supply Air Temperature Setpoint.
  - `DSP`: Discharge Static Pressure.
  - `DSP_SP`: Setpoint for the discharge static pressure.
  - `SA_CFM`: Supply Air flow rate in cubic feet per minute.
  - `CHW_Vlv_Pos`: Position of the chilled water valve (0-100%).
  - `SAF_VFD_Speed`: Commanded speed to the Supply Air Fan VFD.
  - `RPM`: Revolutions per minute of a fan.
  - `FAN_POWER`: Measured power consumption of the fan.

### AHU Sub-Tables
The following tables provide detailed, time-series data for specific AHU components.

#### AHU Chilled Water Table (Pattern: `...__chw_table`)
- Description: Contains data related to the chilled water coil in the Air Handling Unit.
- Columns:
    - `measured_time`: Timestamp of the reading.
    - `CHW_In_Temp`: Chilled Water Inlet Temperature.
    - `CHW_Out_Temp`: Chilled Water Outlet Temperature.
    - `CHW_Vlv_Pos`: Position of the chilled water valve (0-100%).

#### AHU Fan Power Table (Pattern: `...__fan_power_table`)
- Description: Provides detailed information on the fan's power consumption.
- Columns:
    - `measured_time`: Timestamp of the reading.
    - `SA_CFM`: Supply Air flow rate in cubic feet per minute.
    - `DSP`: Discharge Static Pressure.
    - `FAN_POWER`: Measured power consumption of the fan.
    - `FAN_POWER_Error`: The error between the estimated and measured fan power.
    - `Fault_Status`: A flag indicating a fault in the fan system.

#### AHU RPM Table (Pattern: `...__rpm_table`)
- Description: Records the speed of the supply air fan.
- Columns:
    - `measured_time`: Timestamp of the reading.
    - `SA_CFM`: Supply Air flow rate in cubic feet per minute.
    - `DSP`: Discharge Static Pressure.
    - `RPM`: Revolutions per minute of the fan.

### Detailed Chiller Data (Table: `ch_010001b00000_om_p`)
- Description: Key-value table for detailed chiller thermodynamics. Use `param_id` and `param_value`.
- Columns: id, measured_time, param_id, param_value, created_at, modified_at, measured_time_calculated
- Key Parameters (`param_id`):
  - `CHW_In_Temp`: Chilled Water Inlet Temperature.
  - `CHW_Out_Temp`: Chilled Water Outlet Temperature.
  - `CH_Out_Temp_SP`: Chiller Outlet Temperature Setpoint.
  - `CDW_In_Temp`: Condenser Water Inlet Temperature.
  - `CDW_Out_Temp`: Condenser Water Outlet Temperature.
  - `Cmp_Dis_Pre`: Compressor Discharge Pressure.
  - `Cmp_Suc_Pre`: Compressor Suction Pressure.
  - `Dis_SuperHeat`: Discharge Superheat.
  - `amps`: Amperes (electrical current drawn).
  - `Fault_Status`: A flag indicating a system fault.

### Chiller Modeling Tables (Pattern: `ch_...__[measurement]_table`)
- Description: Traditional tables for chiller fault detection, with columns like `Amps_Estimate`, `Amps_Error`, `Fault_Status`.
- Columns: measured_time, CW_IPT, CW_OPT, CD_WET, amps, Disc_pres, Suct_pres, ..._Estimate, ..._Error, Fault_Status

### Pump Status Tables (e.g., `pu_0010b1_om_p`, `secpu_000bb2_om_p`)
- Description: Key-value tables for simple pump status.
- Columns: id, measured_time, param_id, param_value, created_at, modified_at
- Key Parameters (`param_id`):
  - `Pri_Pmp_On_Off`: Primary Pump On/Off
  - `Pri_Pmp_Trip_SS`: Primary Pump Trip Status
  - `Sec_Pmp_On_Off`: Secondary Pump On/Off
  - `Sec_Pmp_Trip_SS`: Secondary Pump Trip Status
